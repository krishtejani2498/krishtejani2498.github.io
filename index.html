Calculator Pro — Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Calculator Pro ("we", "our", "the App"). This app respects your privacy and does not collect any personal information. The app works completely offline and does not require any internet connection.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We do not collect:

  • Name, email, or phone number
  • Photos, media, or files
  • Contacts or messages
  • Location data (precise or approximate)
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Any personally identifiable information

3. App Functionality

Calculator Pro is a simple calculator application designed to perform mathematical calculations. The app works completely offline and does not require internet access. All features function locally on your device.

4. Calculation History

All calculation history is stored locally on your device only. This data is saved solely for your convenience and never leaves your device. We do not upload, sync, or back up this data to any server.

5. Permissions

Calculator Pro does not request any permissions from your device:

  • No access to storage
  • No access to contacts
  • No access to location
  • No access to camera or microphone
  • No access to network or internet

6. Third-Party Services

Calculator Pro does not use any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Tracking SDKs
  • Cloud services

7. Data Sharing

We do not share any data, since we do not collect user information. No data is transmitted from your device to our servers or any third parties.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is safe for all age groups, including children. Since we do not collect any data, the app fully complies with children's privacy protection guidelines.

9. Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal information, there is no risk of data misuse or leakage. All calculation data remains on your device and is under your control.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Your continued use of the App after any update means that you accept the revised policy.
